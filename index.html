About Me

I'm Kival. An aspiring computer science student with a passion for science and technology.

I prefer command lines anyway.

I do freelance development on the side on sites like Freelancer and Upwork. That ranges from automation tools to websites and beyond. Generally, those sites aren't great for smaller developers like me.

On top of that, I am a massive Open Source Enthusiast, with most of the tools I work with being Open Source. And I try to contribute to the community where possible.

I like mathematics, statistics and computer science. Not just because it's interesting, but because it lets me test my limits. And I like that (most of the time).

It makes sense that I'm majoring in Computer Science and Mathematics.

After Undergrad, I want to further my tertiary education with a Masters and PhD, possibly majoring in Machine Learning/AI or Cybersecurity.
